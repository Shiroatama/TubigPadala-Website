Privacy Policy for TubigPadala

Effective Date: November 13, 2025

Last Updated: November 13, 2025

1. Introduction

TubigPadala ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our water delivery app.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Phone Number: Used for account creation and authentication via SMS OTP
  • Name: To identify you in orders and deliveries
  • Address: For accurate water delivery
  • GPS Location: Optional - to pinpoint exact delivery location on map
  • Delivery Notes: Optional - landmarks or special instructions

2.2 Technical Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system
  • Push Notification Token: To send order status updates
  • App Usage Data: For error tracking and app improvements

2.3 Order Information

  • Water type and quantity ordered
  • Order timestamps and status
  • Payment method (Cash on Delivery)
  • Delivery photos (proof of delivery)
  • Payment status (paid/unpaid)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process and deliver your water orders
  • Send you order status notifications
  • Authenticate your account via SMS OTP
  • Show your location to suppliers for delivery
  • Track payment status and unpaid balances
  • Provide delivery proof photos
  • Improve our service and fix technical issues

4. Information Sharing

4.1 With Suppliers

When you place an order, we share with the assigned supplier:

  • Your name and phone number
  • Delivery address and GPS coordinates
  • Order details (water type, quantity)
  • Delivery notes

4.2 Third-Party Services

We use the following services:

  • Supabase: Database and authentication (USA servers)
  • Semaphore API: SMS OTP delivery (Philippines)
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging: Push notifications
  • Cloudinary: Delivery photo storage
  • Sentry: Error tracking (anonymized)
  • Google Maps: Location services

4.3 We Never Share

We will never sell your personal information to third parties.

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Data stored securely in Supabase (encrypted at rest)
  • SMS OTP codes expire after 5 minutes
  • Location data stored as coordinates only
  • Photos stored securely on Cloudinary
  • Regular security audits performed

6. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Kept until you request deletion
  • Order History: Kept indefinitely for business records
  • OTP Codes: Deleted after verification or 5 minutes
  • Delivery Photos: Kept for 1 year for dispute resolution

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account
  • Opt-out of push notifications (in app settings)
  • Withdraw location permission (in device settings)

8. Children's Privacy

TubigPadala is not intended for users under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted in-app and on this page. Continued use means acceptance of changes.
